# Monthly Budget Draft — Version A (Early Draft)

This is an early draft of my personal monthly household budget, put together
while I was still tracking spending manually.

## Grocery and Food Spending

Based on my first two months of tracking, my monthly grocery budget is
**$400**. This covers all supermarket trips for a single person, not
including dining out or takeout, which are tracked separately under
entertainment.

## Other Categories

- Rent: $1,450/month
- Utilities: $120/month
- Transportation: $150/month
- Entertainment/dining out: $200/month

This draft is superseded by a later revision once I had more complete data.
